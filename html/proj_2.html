Currency Design

During my time at M&T one of the software projects that I mainly worked on was the bank's new design system, Currency Design. A design system is a series of components that can be reused in different combinations. A Design System is complied of three main parts writing, web devolopment and design assets. While working on this project I was able to hone in my UX engineering skills. Some of the products and langauges I used during this project are; Sketch, Invison, JavaScript, Angular, React, HTML, CSS, and Sass.
